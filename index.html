About Us

Florida's premier garage sale experts

Your Upscale Thrift Experience

Florida Garage Sales is more than just a thrift shop — we're treasure hunters. We scour garage sales across the Sunshine State to find quality new and used items at incredible values.

Whether you're looking for rare Pokemon cards, vintage sports memorabilia, retro VHS tapes, or everyday items at a fraction of retail, we've got you covered.
