We're on the Blockchain

Florida Garage Sales is proudly built on XPR Network — the future of fast, free, and secure payments

What is Blockchain?

Think of blockchain as a digital ledger that everyone can see but no one can cheat. Every transaction is permanently recorded across thousands of computers worldwide — no banks, no middlemen, no hidden fees. It's the same technology behind Bitcoin, but newer blockchains like XPR Network are faster, cheaper, and more user-friendly.

⚡

Lightning Fast

Transactions confirm in under 1 second. No waiting days for bank transfers or payment processing. Your purchase is confirmed instantly on the blockchain.

💸

Zero Fees

XPR Network has no gas fees or transaction costs. Unlike Ethereum or Bitcoin where fees can be $5-$50+, XPR transactions are completely free. More money stays in your pocket.

🔒

Secure & Transparent

Every transaction is recorded on a public ledger that can't be altered. No chargebacks, no fraud. You can verify any payment on the XPR Explorer.

🌐

Easy to Use

No complicated crypto wallets or seed phrases. XPR Network uses WebAuth — sign in with your browser just like any website. Human-readable account names instead of long wallet addresses.

Why XPR Network Over Other Blockchains?

✓

No gas fees — Bitcoin and Ethereum charge $1-$50+ per transaction. XPR Network is always free.

✓

Sub-second finality — Bitcoin takes 10-60 minutes. Ethereum takes 15+ seconds. XPR confirms in under 1 second.

✓

Human-readable names — Your wallet is @yourname, not 0x7a3b9c... Makes sending payments simple and mistake-free.

✓

Browser-based wallet — No MetaMask or app downloads. WebAuth works right in your browser with biometric security.

✓

Eco-friendly — XPR uses Delegated Proof of Stake, consuming a fraction of the energy that Bitcoin mining requires.

✓

Built-in identity — KYC-verified accounts mean a safer, more trusted ecosystem for buyers and sellers.
